## Why Profiles Matter

The same weather has different business meaning for each venue. Rain may hurt a zoo day more than an indoor pool day. Heat may help Aquapark Fala more than Orientarium. The backend encodes these differences so future screens can show venue-specific recommendations instead of generic forecasts.
